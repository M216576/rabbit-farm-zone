Fun Facts About Rabbits

Discover the wonderful world of rabbits!

  • Rabbits can rotate ears 180 deegrees to detect sounds from various directions.
  • They have a nearly 360-degrees field of vision.
  • Rabbits can run up to 45 miles per hour in short bursts.
  • There are over 30 species of rabbits found arround the world.

Rabbits are small mammals that belong to the family Leporidae.
